BMI Calculator





Values classification of body mass index
(BMI):

  • BMI less than 18.5: Underweight. This may indicate malnutrition or other problems with health.
  • BMI from 18.5 to 24.9: Normal weight. In this range, the weight is considered to be within healthy limits.
  • BMI from 25.0 to 29.9: Overweight. In this category there is some risk of developing related to obese diseases.
  • BMI from 30.0 to 34.9:Obesity I degree. The risk of developing diseases associated with obesity, much increased.
  • BMI from 35.0 to 39.9: Obesity II degree. The risk of developing serious diseases such as like diabetes and cardiovascular diseases, very high.
  • BMI 40 and above: Severe obesity (III degree obesity). High risk of developing serious illnesses and complications.
